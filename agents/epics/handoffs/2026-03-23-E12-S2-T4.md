## Downgrade Data-Preservation Rules

1. **Data is NEVER deleted on downgrade** — `preserveData: true` on every policy
2. **Commerce modules** (catalog, ordering, bookings, loyalty, quotes): `read-only` policy
3. **Content modules** (content, portfolio): `archived` policy
4. **Grace period**: 14 days by default
5. **During grace period**: lost modules are read-only (reads allowed, writes blocked)
6. **After grace period**: lost module data remains accessible in read-only mode

## Grace Period Behavior

- Status: `"grace_period"` on `TenantSubscriptionContext`
- `gracePeriodEnd`: ISO date when grace period expires
- Writes to non-entitled modules are blocked during grace period
- Entitled modules continue to work normally during grace period
