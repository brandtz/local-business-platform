## Usage-Limit Thresholds and Notification Triggers

### Soft Limit (Warning)
- Triggers when `currentUsage >= softLimit`
- Operations are **allowed** to proceed
- Warning notification emitted with remaining count before hard limit
- Message: "You are approaching your limit for {type}. {N} remaining before hard limit."

### Hard Limit (Block)
- Triggers when `currentUsage >= hardLimit`
- Operations are **blocked**
- Error notification emitted
- Message: "You have reached the maximum limit of {N} for {type}. Please upgrade your plan to continue."

### No Limit Configured
- Treated as unlimited — operations always allowed

### Notification Shape
```json
{
  "tenantId": "t-1",
  "limitType": "orders_per_month",
  "status": "soft-limit-warning",
  "currentUsage": 100,
  "hardLimit": 150,
  "message": "You are approaching your limit...",
  "emittedAt": "2026-03-23T00:00:00Z"
}
```
