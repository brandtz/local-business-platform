## Assumptions and Risks

- Assumptions made:
  - Rollback is always two-phase: initiate (promoted → rollback-pending) then complete (rollback-pending → rolled-back)
  - Managed subdomain is restored on successful rollback (via DomainPromotionWorkflowService.completeRollback)
  - Rollback must be automatic and immediate on post-promotion validation failure
- Risks introduced or still open:
  - If the two-phase rollback fails between phases, the domain is in rollback-pending state and requires manual intervention
  - Automatic trigger mechanism depends on Epic 9 health monitoring and Epic 10 operational runbooks
- Invariants changed: none
- Invariants preserved: Failed activation preserves the prior live configuration — no broken states
