## Worker Dispatch Pattern
1. Upload creates artifact + job (pending)
2. Job dispatched to queue via `ImportJobQueue.enqueue()`
3. Worker picks up job, calls registered `ImportJobProcessor.process()`
4. On success: job transitions to `staged` or `completed`
5. On failure: job transitions to `failed` with reason
6. Retry: validates count < maxRetries, resets to `pending`, increments retryCount, re-dispatches

## Retry Semantics
- **Max retries:** 3 (configurable via `ImportJobRetryConfig`)
- **Backoff:** Exponential: `min(5000 * 2^attempt, 60000)` ms
  - Attempt 0: 5s, 1: 10s, 2: 20s, 3: 40s (capped at 60s)
- **Idempotency:** Staged output is overwritten on retry (no duplication)
- **Resumability:** `listResumableJobs()` returns jobs stuck in "processing" for re-dispatch after worker restart
