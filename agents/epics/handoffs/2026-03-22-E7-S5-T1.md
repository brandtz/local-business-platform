## Key Design Decisions

- Customer identity is scoped per tenant via the CustomerProfile model (tenantId + userId).
- The same email can have separate CustomerProfile records in different tenants.
- UserId is shared across tenants (derived from email) to support cross-tenant identity mapping.
- Registration creates a pseudo userId — in production this integrates with E2 session-lifecycle.
- Duplicate email within the same tenant is rejected.
