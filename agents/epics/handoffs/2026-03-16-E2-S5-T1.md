## Assumptions and Risks

- Assumptions made:
  - platform writes and impersonation start are the highest-trust current platform actions and should require stronger freshness than ordinary reads
  - tenant settings, staff, payment, and refund operations are the highest-risk near-term tenant actions and should be the first tenant-side step-up triggers
- Risks introduced or still open:
  - the policy currently defines requirements but not how MFA state is stored or proven, so later implementation must preserve these reasons and freshness windows
  - future product decisions may add more privileged operations, which should extend the shared operation vocabulary rather than bypassing it in individual endpoints
